# QuestBoard — Logo Generation Prompts

**Project:** QuestBoard — A Gamified Q&A Platform for STEM Problem Solving  
**Use:** App icon, splash screen, app bar, report cover, promotional material

> Paste each prompt into your preferred AI image generator.
> Recommended tools: **Midjourney**, **Adobe Firefly**, **DALL·E 3**, or **Ideogram** (Ideogram handles text in logos best).

---

## Design Direction

**What QuestBoard is:**
A gamified Q&A platform for STEM students. Users earn points, win bounties, and level up by helping each other solve math, physics, and coding problems.

**Logo personality:**
- Competitive but academic — like a game guild badge for smart people
- Dark, bold, premium — not playful or childish
- Feels like it belongs next to apps like Discord, Codeforces, or Duolingo (dark edition)

**Core color palette:**
- Primary: Electric purple `#7C3AED`
- Secondary: Neon cyan `#22D3EE`
- Accent: Gold `#F59E0B`
- Background: Deep navy `#0D1117`

## Prompt 1 — Primary Logo (Icon + Wordmark)

```
Design a professional app logo for "QuestBoard", a dark-themed gamified STEM Q&A platform for students and competitive programmers.

ICON CONCEPT:
A bold shield shape as the primary icon. Inside the shield:
- A stylized letter "Q" formed by combining a question mark "?" with a lightning bolt cutting through it diagonally — the lightning bolt replaces the dot of the question mark. This symbolizes both "question" and "instant energy/power".
- The shield has a subtle inner glow and a thin border.

COLORS:
- Shield body: deep dark navy #0D1117 or very dark purple
- Lightning bolt / Q shape: gradient from electric purple #7C3AED on the left to neon cyan #22D3EE on the right
- Thin shield border: electric purple #7C3AED with a soft outer neon glow
- Small gold star or diamond accent shape in the top-right corner of the shield: gold #F59E0B — represents achievement/points

WORDMARK (beside or below the icon):
- "QuestBoard" in bold geometric sans-serif font (similar to Space Grotesk or Rajdhani)
- "Quest" in white bold
- "Board" in electric purple #7C3AED bold
- Slight letter spacing, clean and modern

STYLE:
- Dark background #0D1117 for the composition
- The icon has a subtle drop shadow and a very soft radial purple-cyan glow behind it
- Overall feel: premium gaming app badge — similar to Discord or Valorant branding but academic
- Vector-clean, sharp edges, no gradients that look muddy
- No photorealism — flat with subtle glow effects only

OUTPUT:
- Transparent or dark navy background
- Icon should work at small sizes (48px app icon) and large sizes (1024px store icon)
```

## Prompt 2 — Icon Only (App Icon / Favicon)

```
Design a standalone app icon for "QuestBoard", a gamified STEM Q&A mobile app for students.

CONCEPT:
A bold geometric shield icon, designed to look great at small sizes (48x48px) as a mobile app icon.

ICON DETAILS:
- Shape: classic shield / crest shape with slightly pointed bottom, flat top — like a game guild emblem
- Inside the shield: a stylized "Q?" symbol — a question mark where the curved part is formed by a clean bold arc and the tail is replaced by a short lightning bolt pointing downward-right. The symbol is centered inside the shield.
- The symbol uses a left-to-right gradient: electric purple #7C3AED to neon cyan #22D3EE
- A tiny 5-pointed gold star #F59E0B sits in the upper-right inside corner of the shield — small enough to not dominate but visible as a detail

SHIELD STYLING:
- Shield fill: very dark navy #0D1117 or dark charcoal
- Shield border/outline: 3px electric purple #7C3AED with a soft outer glow (bloom effect)
- Subtle inner gradient from dark purple at top to pure dark at bottom inside the shield

BACKGROUND (for the icon tile):
- Rounded square app icon format (like iOS/Android icons)
- Background: deep navy #0D1117 with a very subtle radial glow in purple at the center behind the shield

STYLE:
- Bold, minimal, iconic — recognizable at 48px
- No text — icon only
- Flat design with subtle glow effects, not 3D
- Clean vector aesthetic
- The shield should feel prestigious and powerful, not cute or cartoony
```

## Prompt 3 — Alternate Concept (Book + Circuit)

```
Design an alternate logo icon for "QuestBoard", a gamified STEM Q&A platform.

ALTERNATE CONCEPT:
Instead of a shield, use an open book as the base shape. The book is stylized and geometric — not realistic.

ICON DETAILS:
- An open book viewed from a slight top-down angle, pages spread open
- The left page has a "?" symbol on it in electric purple #7C3AED
- The right page has a simple circuit board trace pattern or a mathematical sigma symbol "Σ" in neon cyan #22D3EE
- A lightning bolt erupts upward from the center spine of the book, in a gold-to-purple gradient #F59E0B to #7C3AED — representing knowledge becoming power
- The book outline and spine: white or very light gray, bold strokes

COLORS:
- Book body: dark slate #161B22 fill, white/light outline
- Left page symbol: electric purple #7C3AED
- Right page symbol: neon cyan #22D3EE
- Lightning bolt: gold #F59E0B fading to purple #7C3AED at the tip
- Background: deep navy #0D1117

STYLE:
- Geometric, flat design with subtle glow on the lightning bolt
- Bold enough to work as a small app icon
- No text
- Clean, modern, academic-meets-gaming aesthetic
- The lightning bolt is the focal point — it should have a subtle radial glow at its base where it meets the book spine
```

## Prompt 4 — Splash Screen Version (Logo with Tagline)

```
Design a splash screen logo lockup for "QuestBoard", a dark-themed gamified STEM Q&A mobile app.

COMPOSITION (vertical, centered, for a 390x844px mobile screen):
- Background: deep dark navy #0D1117 with a very subtle dot-grid pattern at 5% opacity
- A soft radial glow in the center of the screen — purple #7C3AED at the core fading to transparent — like a spotlight behind the logo

LOGO ELEMENTS (stacked vertically, centered):
1. ICON (top): The QuestBoard shield icon — bold shield shape with the Q-lightning-bolt symbol inside, purple-to-cyan gradient symbol, gold star accent, purple glowing border. Size: 100x100dp centered.
2. APP NAME (below icon, 16dp gap): "QuestBoard" in bold geometric sans-serif 32sp. "Quest" in white, "Board" in electric purple #7C3AED. Slight letter spacing 0.5.
3. TAGLINE (below name, 8dp gap): "Level up your problem-solving" in muted gray #8B949E 15sp italic, centered.
4. LOADING INDICATOR (bottom of screen, separate from logo group): a thin horizontal progress bar 120dp wide, centered, purple-to-cyan gradient fill, animated left-to-right — show it at 60% filled state for the static design.

STYLE:
- The glow behind the shield should be the most dramatic element — like the logo is lit from behind
- Everything else is minimal — dark background, only the logo and tagline
- No other UI elements — pure splash screen
- The overall composition should feel like loading into a premium game
```

## Prompt 5 — Wordmark Only (for Report Cover / Documentation)

```
Design a text wordmark logo for "QuestBoard" suitable for a university project report cover page.

WORDMARK DETAILS:
- Text: "QuestBoard"
- Font style: bold geometric sans-serif — similar to Space Grotesk Bold, Rajdhani Bold, or Orbitron
- "Quest" — white or very light gray
- "Board" — electric purple #7C3AED
- No font mixing — same typeface, just color difference between the two words
- Slight letter spacing: 1-2px between characters for a premium feel
- No italic

ACCOMPANYING ICON:
- The QuestBoard shield icon placed to the left of the wordmark, vertically centered
- Icon size: same cap-height as the text
- Total lockup: [shield icon] [Quest][Board]

SUBTITLE LINE (optional, below the wordmark):
- "A Gamified Q&A Platform for STEM Problem Solving" in muted gray, same font but regular weight, 40% of the wordmark size
- Centered below the main wordmark

BACKGROUND OPTIONS — generate two versions:
1. Dark version: deep navy #0D1117 background — for digital use and app store
2. Light version: white #FFFFFF background — for print and the report PDF cover page

STYLE:
- Clean, professional, minimal
- No glow effects — this is for a formal document
- The purple-white color split on "QuestBoard" is the only stylistic element needed
- Vector-clean — must look sharp at any size
```

## Tips for Best Results

- **Ideogram** handles text inside logos better than any other AI tool — use it for Prompts 1 and 5 where the wordmark matters.
- **Midjourney** produces the best aesthetic quality for the icon-only prompts — add `--ar 1:1 --style raw` at the end.
- If the generator adds unwanted text or extra elements, add to any prompt: `no extra text, no watermarks, no additional symbols, no realistic textures`.
- Generate at least 4 variations per prompt and pick the best — AI logo generation rarely nails it on the first try.
- Once you pick a direction, export as SVG or high-res PNG (1024x1024 minimum) and use Figma or Canva to fine-tune colors to exactly `#7C3AED` and `#22D3EE`.
